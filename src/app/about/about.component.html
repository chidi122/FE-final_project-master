About Serena

Hey there, I'm Serena Montgomery, a wildlife photographer with a passion for capturing the untamed beauty of our planet. My journey into photography began unexpectedly, sparked by a backpacking trip through Africa in my early twenties. It was there, amidst the breathtaking landscapes and majestic wildlife, that I discovered the transformative power of photography.

Fast forward a few years, and here I am, living out my wildest dreams as a National Geographic photographer. Every day, I'm privileged to roam the globe, seeking out the most remote and awe-inspiring corners of our world to document its wonders.

My work with National Geographic has taken me to some incredible places, from the misty jungles of China to the vast savannas of Africa. Armed with my camera and fueled by curiosity, I've had the privilege of witnessing some truly extraordinary moments in the natural world.

Through my work, I hope to inspire others to see the beauty and wonder that surrounds us and to join me in the effort to preserve and protect it for future generations. So here's to the wild ones, the ones who roam free and defy our expectations. And here's to the power of photography to capture their spirit and share it with the world.
